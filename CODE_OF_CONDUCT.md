# Code of Conduct

Keep discussion practical, respectful, and focused on improving the tool.

## Be Constructive

- Be direct, but do not be rude.
- Keep feedback specific enough that someone can act on it.
- Do not post private data.

## Not Allowed

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Secrets, credentials, private files, screenshots of private systems, or non-public data.
- Repeated off-topic or bad-faith participation.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues or pull requests; or block accounts when needed.
